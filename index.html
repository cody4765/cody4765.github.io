Oregon Tech and the University of Portland have joined forces with the AFROTC!

Oregon Tech has joined forces with University of Portland (UP) for a “cross-town” agreement making Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps (AFROTC) possible if you attend the Portland-Metro campus (or you are an Online student who lives in the Portland-Metro area). This agreement allows you to attend Oregon Tech while simultaneously taking AFROTC classes at the University of Portland. The AFROTC General Military Course and/or Professional Officer Course is available to qualified students of Oregon Tech who desire to earn appointments as commissioned officers in the United States Air Force.

AFROTC is a 4-year program. If you are admitted as a freshman or will be a sophomore student with four years until graduation, AFROTC could work for you! AFROTC scholarship opportunities are outstanding. While scholarships favor freshmen who have never attended college, there are also scholarship opportunities available for other students in the program. Officers with Engineering, Cybersecurity and other technical degrees are in high demand in the Air Force and Space Force, so this partnership with Oregon Tech and UP could offer significant scholarship opportunities for prospective cadets. AFROTC schedule at University of Portland

Freshman and sophomore courses and training are held at the University of Portland campus and are free for Oregon Tech students accepted into AFROTC Detachment 695.

AS101 Section A / 1 course hour AS101 Section B / 1 course hour AS111 / 2 course hours (NOTE: with COVID mitigation, students at universities other than University of Portland only attended the events listed here for leadership lab and physical training)

AS201 Section A / 1 course hour AS201 Section B / 1 course hour AS211 / 2 course hours (NOTE: with COVID mitigation, students at universities other than University of Portland only attended the events listed here for leadership lab and physical training)

Each student would need to register for one of the 101 or 102 section offerings AND the 111 or 211 offering. As an example, Freshmen could register for AS101B and AS111 for the fall term.

AFROTC Detachment 695 information can be found here: Welcome to AFROTC Det 695 | University of Portland (up.edu) Det 695 also has a blog that can be found here:https://sites.up.edu/afrotc/
